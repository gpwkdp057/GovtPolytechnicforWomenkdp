Admission Details






details

      Admission Procedure to join in Polytechnics:


      •  The application submitted for POLYCET-2024 itself is an application for admission under both local and non-local categories. Hence the candidates are advised to fill the POLYCET-2024 application form carefully. (No separate application is prescribed for admission.)

      •  The rank cards can be downloaded from https://polycetap.nic.in

      •  Securing rank at POLYCET-2024 does not entitle any right for the candidate to be considered for admission unless he/she satisfies all other conditions prescribed for the purpose.

      •  Call letters to attend the admission camp will not be sent.

      •  Hall Ticket of POLYCET- 2024 is to be preserved and produced at the time of Admission

      •  The detailed program of admission will be notified in the leading newspapers, after the declaration of the results. Program of Admission will also be available in all the Polytechnics. Candidates are advised to watch for the same. Admission Notification itself is to be treated as a call letter.

      •  For the purpose of admissions, institutions offering diploma courses are classified into regional institutions, state-wide institutions.

      •  85% of the available seats in each regional institution [Government / Aided Polytechnic/ Institution] shall be filled by candidates belonging to the respective University area of the two University areas namely
        (1) Andhra University Area,
        (2) Sri Venkateswara University Area.
        The remaining15%unreserved seats shall be made available to Local candidates of both A.P.& T.S. and non-local candidates in A.P. & T.S. depending upon their ranking at POLYCET-2024. However, candidates who wish to opt 15% unreserved seats in AP from Telangana state, those candidates shall appear POLYCET 2024 conducted by Andhra Pradesh.

      •  85% of the seats in each State-wide institution/course shall be filled by Candidates belonging to the three University areas namely (1) Andhra University Area, (2) Osmania University Area and

      •  (3) Sri Venkateswara University Area in the ratio of 42:36:22 respectively and the balance 15% seats as un-reserved

      •  Admissions into Private, unaided, Minority Polytechnics are subject to issue of Government orders.

      •  Candidates may note that those admitted in the following State Wide Institutions are
        eligible for Multi-Point Entry and Credit System(MPEC).
        (i) Govt. Institute of Printing Technology, Secunderabad.
        (ii) Govt. Institute of Leather Technology, Hyderabad.
        (iii) Govt. Institute of Ceramic Technology, Gudur, Nellore Dist.
        (iv) Govt. Institute of Textile Technology, Guntur.
        (v) Govt. Institute of Electronics, Secunderabad.

      •  Candidates who qualified in POLYCET–2024 and have passed 10 + 2, 10 + ITI, 10 + Intermediate (Vocational) or 3-year Degree Courses in Sciences are eligible for Multi Point Entry and Credit System (MPEC). Such of these candidates shall get certain credits for certain subjects by means of exemption, as decided by an expert committee, as they have already studied in 10 + 2, 10 + ITI, 10 + Intermediate (Vocational) and 3-Year Degree Course in Sciences, as the case may be, by opting for extra credits, a student can finish the Diploma earlier than the stipulated period.

      •  Hostel accommodation is not guaranteed in any Institution.

      •  Reservation of Seats:

      •  Reservation of seats in favour of SC/ST/BC (A, B, C, D, E) Candidates shall be made in non- minority institutions as per the following percentages.

      •  OPEN COMPETITION-50%, SC - 15 % , ST - 6 % , BC - 29% (BC ‘A’ - 7%, BC ‘B’ - 10%, BC

      •  ‘C’ - 1%, BC ‘D’ - 7%,BC ‘E’-4%)

      •  The list of various communities of SC/ST/BC approved by Government of Andhra Pradesh is given in Annexure-IV.

      •  For Residential Polytechnics at Madanapalle, Rajahmundry (Andhra Pradesh),), the reservations are:

      •  S.C. - 75% S.T.-05% B.C.-10% O.C. -10%

      •  For Residential Polytechnics at Paderu, Srisailam and Yetapaka near Bhadrachalam (AP) the reservations are:

      •  S.C.-18% S.T.- 75% B.C.-05% O.C. -02%

      •  All the seats are reserved for BOYS ONLY

      •  For Government Minority Polytechnics at Kurnool, Guntur (A.P) and Badangpet Hyderabad (women) (Telangana) all the seats are reserved for minority candidates.

      •  Candidates claiming to be considered under Minority Community should submit Xerox Copy of school leaving certificate issued by concerned Head of Institution to that effect at the time of counseling or submit Transfer Certificate proof containing the religion column.

      • 33.33% of total seats in all non-minority Co-Educational institutions are reserved for girl candidates in each reservation category. If sufficient numbers of candidates are not available to fill the seats reserved for girls, they will be offered to the boys of the same category.

      •  Reservation for Anglo Indians: Two (2) seats are reserved in polytechnics in favour of the candidates belonging to the Anglo Indian community from the academic year 1994- 95 onwards vide G.O.Ms. No. 173 Edn. (TE-1) Dept. dated 15-6-1994. Candidates claiming reservation in Anglo Indian Community shall produce a community certificate issued by Revenue official not below the rank of Deputy Tahasildar of their place ofresidence.

      •  Reservation of seats for NCC, Sports and Games, Physically Challenged (Handicapped) and Children of Armed Personnel

      •  Horizontal reservation in each of the reservation category Open/SC/ST/BC to the extent of percentages mentioned is applicable.

        • (i) NCC (National Cadet Corps):
        (ii) Reservation to the extent of 1% shall be available for candidates of this category. The priority for consideration will be as per GO.Ms.No.21 HE (TE-1) Dept., Dt.13- 05- 2002 [Refer Annexure VIII].
        (iii) SPORTS:
        (iv) Reservation to the extent of 0.5% (½%) shall be available for candidates of this category. Certificates issued by various organizations constituted by Central / State Government to conduct the event will alone are considered under this category. The participation in games and Sports before the qualifying Examinations will only be considered. The priority under this Category will be fixed as per the rules in vogue at the time of admissions.       •  Inter conversion of seats among the special reservation categories is not applicable. If sufficient numbers of candidates are available to fill the seats reserved for special reservation category, they shall be filled with candidates of general pool of that reservation category.
